About NebulaHub

NebulaHub is a personal project built and maintained by an individual developer. The site serves as a hub for browser-based games and other interactive content, all organized into one simple, easy-to-use interface.

The goal of NebulaHub is to provide a clean, distraction-light experience for discovering and launching games and other web apps. The site itself is a static front-end hosted on GitHub Pages and does not run its own game servers.

How NebulaHub works

Most games and experiences on NebulaHub are loaded as standalone HTML files or embedded web content. Some games are created by third-party developers and are only linked or embedded here for convenience. Where possible, credit is given to the original creators or rights holders.

NebulaHub does not claim ownership of third-party trademarks, game assets, or intellectual property. All such rights remain with their respective owners.
